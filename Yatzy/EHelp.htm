Let's assume that you now know Yahtzee by heart.
We will now describe what makes Yatzy different:

Roll Category Points in Yahtzee Points in Yatzy
2-2-2-3-3 Full house 25 14
2-2-6-6-1 2 pairs NA 16
6-6-4-3-2 1 pair NA 12
1-2-3-4-5 Large straight 40 0
1-2-3-4-5 Small straight 30 15
1-2-3-4-4 Small straight 30 0
6-6-6-3-2 3 of a kind 23 18
4-4-4-4-6 4 of a kind 22 16
2-3-4-5-6 Large straight 40 20

To sum it up, there are two new categories, 1 pair and 2 pairs, Chance and Yahtzee/Yatzy are unchanged. There are subtle and not so subtle changes to the scoring in the remaining categories.

The bonus is up from 35 to 50 points.

The straights are completely revamped. Less points and harder to come by.

Maxiyatzy is Yatzy with six dice + an interesting twist. If you score early, i.e. before using all three rolls, the remaing rolls are carried to the next round. This makes it possible to achieve near perfect scores with a bit of luck in the beginning.

Balut is derived from Yatzy but with more emphasis on strategy. Look here www.balut.org
